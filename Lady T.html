First Lady, Pray Warrior  & Entrepreneur

Virtuous, Strong, Honorable, Wise, Understanding, Patient, Friendly  and Energetic are just some of the words used to describe this humble Woman of God. She is affectionately known as Lady T to the women of Bethlehem City of Faith.  Tomecka Eichelberger is the First Lady of Bethlehem City of Faith (COGIC) in Riverdale, Georgia.  She is wife and entrepreneur and a dedicated mother to their 3 children. They have been married for twenty years and from this union they have been blessed with one girl and two boys - Jamiya LaStephanie, Jameson Jr. and  Joseph.  

 

Lady Eichelberger received salvation at the age 23 under Bishop Paul Fortson of Atlanta, Ga. This woman of God made a lasting impression on her husband by the example she set as a Godly woman.  Pastor often testifies how Lady Eichelberger conversion impacted his life so much that it made him turn to God in a greater way. Therefore, because of her example it lead him to where he is today as a man of God. What a testimony to here from this couple.

 Recognized as a praying woman, possessing many spiritual gifts, including the gift of healing and discernment, Lady Eichelberger has traveled extensively, nationally and internationally, declaring the Gospel of Jesus Christ with her husband as a tag team of dunamis power.  In addition, she's the leader of More than Conquerors Women Ministry at Bethlehem City of Faith Church teaching, loving, caring and building up the women in her local church. The ministry was designed to minister to the needs of women from all walks of life. 

 

Lady Eichelberger works diligently with her husband to coordinate the Annual Community Outreach. This program is conducted annually for children in the community as they go back to school.  Love is shown by way of providing food, school supplies, praying, anointing the children, their parents and educational professionals for God to bless their school year.  Lady Eichelberger is a powerful woman of God who serves as an example of a virtuous woman for both the young and old at the Bethlehem City of Faith and abroad.
