Prophet, Pastor and Change Architect

 

Anointed by God Jameson Eichelberger is a vessel being used as a mouth piece of God to speak prophetic revelation and hope into people everywhere no matter the gender, nationality or religious affiliation.  However, before his conversion he lived a destructive lifestyle and was an upcoming recording gangster rap artist. In his music career he, opened up for secular artists such as Triple Six Mafia, Pastor Troy, and Ghetto Mafia.

 

He thought that was his destiny but the Lord had another assignment. The Lord saved and baptized him in the Holy Ghost, what a testimony to hear from tragedy to triumph. The testimony will invoke change and confer hope to the listener. By God's mercy and grace  this young man was delivered from a destructive lifestyle which altered his life's course. Psalms 40:2 declares, "He brought me up also out of a horrible pit. Out of the miry clay, and set my foot upon a rock and established my goings. Then, the Lord spoke to him what an amazing visitation from the Lord to hear from the man of God. The Lord said to him I sanctified thee and I ordained thee a prophet unto the nations."

 

The Lord has given him a very uncommon ministry of teaching, preaching and the demonstration of the spirit of God in accordance with Mark 16:17. He is described as "passionate, powerful, electrifying, prophetic and anointed."  With this unique ability and gift he's recognized as one of the emerging voices in the Kingdom of God.  However, his assertion is he simply loves Jesus and realizes  his existence is only because of God's love, mercy and grace. He has traveled in the U.S. and internationally devoted to ministering the word of God.

 

Pastor Eichelberger served 16 years under the leadership of Bishop Paul Fortson serving as Youth Pastor, Sunday School Teacher, and National Adjutant to the Bishop. Currently, the man of God is in pursuit of a Bachelor degree in Biblical Studies and Leadership. He's the founder & Pastor of Bethlehem City of Faith Church located in Riverdale, Georgia. Also, he is the founder/CEO of JME ministries his personal ministry that has birthed the Prayer Shift prayer call a movement that's sweeping across the nation. Currently Jameson Eichelberger resides in Atlanta Georgia with his wife Tomecka and three children; Jamiya, Jameson Jr. and Joseph.
